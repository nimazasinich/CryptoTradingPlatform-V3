## Understanding Signals

### Score Ranges
- üü¢ **70-100:** Strong signal (high confidence) - Consider entry
- üü° **50-69:** Moderate signal (medium confidence) - Monitor closely
- üî¥ **0-49:** Weak signal (low confidence) - Wait for better setup

### Actions
- üü¢ **BUY:** Bullish signal - potential long entry
- üî¥ **SELL:** Bearish signal - potential short entry
- ‚ö™ **HOLD:** Neutral - no clear direction

### Entry/Exit Levels
- **Entry Price:** Suggested entry point
- **Stop Loss:** Maximum loss level (risk management)
- **TP1, TP2, TP3:** Three take-profit targets (scaled exits)
- **R:R Ratio:** Risk/Reward ratio (e.g., 1:2 = risk 1% to gain 2%)
